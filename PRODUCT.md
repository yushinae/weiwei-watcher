# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Active crypto and derivatives traders who monitor positions, volatility, options chains, account risk, alerts, and trade journals from a dense desktop trading cockpit. They work under time pressure, compare many market signals at once, and need fast scanning more than decorative storytelling.

## Product Purpose

Weiwei React is a trading decision terminal for building, monitoring, and reviewing options and portfolio strategies. Success means a trader can move from market context to strategy construction to risk assessment without losing confidence in the data, controls, or execution path.

## Brand Personality

Operational, precise, and calm. The product should feel like a professional exchange terminal: compact, dark, responsive, and trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style SaaS pages, oversized hero layouts, pale card grids, playful illustration systems, decorative gradients, and low-density dashboards that make trading data harder to compare. Avoid copying any exchange's brand identity, logo, proprietary iconography, or exact product surface.

## Design Principles

- Keep the task surface dense but readable, with clear hierarchy between market context, controls, and analysis.
- Make trading state obvious through consistent color semantics, reserving green/red for market direction and orange for selected or primary actions.
- Prefer direct manipulation and inline controls over modal-heavy workflows.
- Show risk before action: strategy builders should expose cost, payoff, Greeks, and scenario sensitivity as the user edits.
- Preserve user flow with stable layouts, predictable controls, and minimal decorative motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support keyboard focus states, reduced motion preferences, and redundant non-color cues for trading state where practical.
